Terra Boa

Documentary film · original soundtrack

Terra Boa is a documentary film directed by Felipe Rocha, centered on land, memory and ways of inhabiting time. The work approaches its subject through attention rather than explanation.

The original soundtrack was composed as an extension of the film’s rhythm, treating sound as presence and continuity rather than illustration.
