Listening Set

Long-duration listening performance

Listening Set is a live performance format developed by Felipe Rocha, centered on extended listening, reduced gestures and attention to space.

Rather than a concert, the set functions as a listening environment. Sound unfolds slowly, allowing the audience to enter, remain or leave at any point.

Each presentation adapts to the architectural, acoustic and social conditions of the space where it takes place.

Format — live listening performance
Duration — 30 to 90 minutes (variable)
Audience — seated or lying down
Entrance / exit — free
Visual focus — none
Sound — primary material
